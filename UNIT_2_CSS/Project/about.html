About CBUS

CBUS makes college commuting simple and stress - free.

CBUS (College Bus Information System) is designed to make campus commuting easier and more reliable for students and staff.

It provides clear details about bus routes, stops, timings, and driver contacts, all in one place.

With CBUS, you can stay informed about delays, announcements, and changes, ensuring a smooth and stress - free travel experience every day.
